Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

TSPL Simulator ("we", "our", or "us") collects minimal information to provide and improve our service:

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics through Google Analytics
  • TSPL Code: Code entered in the editor is processed locally and on our server for rendering
  • Cookies: We use cookies for preferences (theme, language) and advertising

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the TSPL Simulator service
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google may use cookies and collect data as described in their Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics: For analyzing website traffic

4. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

5. Data Storage

We do not permanently store your TSPL code. All code is processed in real-time and discarded after rendering.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of analytics and advertising cookies

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@tspl-simulator.com
